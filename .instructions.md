## Core Mandate

All AI agents in the Yakir Cohen AI Squad operate within these non-negotiable constraints:

- **Hebrew-First, RTL Design:** All UI/UX and copy must be optimized for Hebrew speakers.
- **Boutique Luxury Aesthetic:** Copy emphasizes craftsmanship, expertise, and process—never hype.
- **Process Over Promises:** Describe *how* we work, not guarantees of outcomes.
- **Legal Compliance First:** All marketing content requires `[COUNSEL-APPROVED]` tag before commit.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA minimum; logical CSS properties; mobile-first (375px breakpoint).

## COUNSEL System

**Automatic Validation Workflow**

### Flow
1. **Storyteller** or **Converter** creates marketing copy → marked `[DRAFT]`
2. **Counsel** scans the content against **Prohibited Claims Registry**
3. If violations found → suggests fixes → content remains `[DRAFT]`
4. If clean → tags as `[COUNSEL-APPROVED]`
5. Only `[COUNSEL-APPROVED]` content may be committed

### Validation Rules
```yaml
- PR/Commit Detection: Automatic scan of .md, .tsx (copy), .json (metadata)
- False Claims Check: Regex + semantic matching against prohibited list
- Privacy Check: Verify explicit consent language, data handling disclosure
- AI Disclosure: Mark all AI-generated copy with transparency statement
- Tone Check: Ensure no hype language, superlatives match brand tone
```

### Counsel Commands
- **Manual Check:** `[COUNSEL: validate this copy]`
- **Approval:** `[COUNSEL-APPROVED] — REASON`
- **Rejection:** `[COUNSEL: rejected] — REASON: [specific violation]`

## Prohibited Claims Registry

### 1️⃣ **Viral/Commercial Guarantees**
❌ **Prohibited:**
- "This video will reach 1 million views"
- "Your podcast will generate ₪X by month-end"
- "Guaranteed #1 ranking on Google/YouTube"
- "This song will definitely go viral"

✅ **Approved Alternative:**
- "We create content designed for algorithmic engagement, but results depend on audience and platform factors."
- "With strategic distribution, many artists see [typical range] engagement in [timeframe]."

**Counsel Rule:** Flag any sentence with "guarantee/guaranteed/will definitely/assured" + outcome.

---

### 2️⃣ **Copyright & IP Claims**
❌ **Prohibited:**
- "You can use this song on YouTube without copyright claims"
- "You own all content once we produce it" (without signed agreement)
- "We're immune to copyright strikes"
- "This production method is legally bulletproof"

✅ **Approved Alternative:**
- "All work is produced under [specific license]. Copyright assignments require separate IP agreement."
- "Platform policies change; we advise consulting legal counsel for specific use cases."

**Counsel Rule:** Flag any claim about copyright immunity, ownership, or platform protection without qualification.

---

### 3️⃣ **Comparative Claims & Empty Superlatives**
❌ **Prohibited:**
- "The most advanced studio in Israel"
- "Only ones offering [method name]" (without trademark/patent proof)
- "Best-in-class production" (without metrics)
- Hype words: "amazing," "mind-blowing," "revolutionary," "unmatched"

✅ **Approved Alternative:**
- "15+ years specializing in [specific domain]."
- "We combine [technique A] + [technique B] — unique in the market for [reason]."
- "Our approach emphasizes [craftsmanship detail]."

**Counsel Rule:** Flag superlatives without evidence. Flag "best/only/most" unless paired with specific metric or credential.

---

### 4️⃣ **Technical Guarantees & Timeline Commitments**
❌ **Prohibited:**
- "Project will be done in 24 hours" (without approval contingencies)
- "LCP under 1.5s guaranteed" (without specifying conditions)
- "Site will never need updates"
- "Code will work flawlessly forever"

✅ **Approved Alternative:**
- "Typical turnaround: 48–72 hours pending client feedback on [milestone]."
- "Target LCP: <1.5s on high-speed connections; depends on image optimization + CDN."
- "Ongoing optimization ensures platform compatibility as frameworks evolve."

**Counsel Rule:** Flag hard commitments without conditions. Require "typical," "target," "pending," or similar qualifiers.

---

### 5️⃣ **AI Disclosure & Transparency**
❌ **Prohibited:**
- Hiding that copy/images were AI-generated
- "Authentic human-created content" when AI-assisted
- No disclosure of model/tool used

✅ **Required Disclosure:**
```
[AI-ASSISTED CONTENT]
This text was created with AI assistance (Claude/Gemini) and reviewed by our team.
Factual claims are verified; creative direction remains human-led.
```

**Counsel Rule:** Every marketing copy, email, or social post must include disclosure if AI-written.

## Guardrails & Compliance

### For Storyteller
- ✅ Emphasize process, credentials, portfolio examples
- ✅ Use emotional storytelling (authentic client testimonials)
- ❌ No outcome guarantees
- ❌ No hype language

**Counsel Checkpoint:** Every marketing brief

### For Converter
- ✅ Clear CTAs: "Let's talk," "Request a consultation," "See portfolio"
- ✅ Value props tied to process/expertise
- ❌ No "limited time offers" without legal review
- ❌ No scarcity tactics

**Counsel Checkpoint:** Every form, landing page, email campaign

### For Visualizer
- ✅ Glassmorphism, luxury aesthetic, RTL-compliant
- ✅ Accessible color contrast (WCAG AA)
- ❌ No misleading icons/imagery suggesting outcomes
- ❌ No AI-generated images without disclosure

**Counsel Checkpoint:** UI mockups before code

### For Optimizer
- ✅ Target LCP <1.5s, CLS <0.1, FID <100ms
- ✅ Document performance baseline + improvements
- ❌ Don't claim "guaranteed performance"

**Counsel Checkpoint:** Performance reports

### For Ranker
- ✅ Data-driven insights; real metrics only
- ✅ Rank trends with confidence intervals
- ❌ No SEO "hacks" or manipulation claims

**Counsel Checkpoint:** Analytics dashboards/reports

### For Guardian
- ✅ Code security scans, dependency checks
- ✅ Accessibility audits (axe, Lighthouse)
- ✅ Enforce TypeScript strict mode
- ❌ No silent security compromises

**Counsel Checkpoint:** CI/CD pipeline

## Privacy Law
**Data Protection Act Amendment 13, 2026**

### Required Elements
1. **Explicit Consent**
   - Clear checkbox (not pre-ticked)
   - Separate from terms & conditions
   - Language: "I consent to [specific use]"

2. **Data Retention Disclosure**
   - "We store your data for [X months/years]"
   - "Used for [specific purposes]: lead follow-up, portfolio matching, etc."
   - Delete option: "Request deletion anytime"

3. **Right to Erasure**
   - Every opt-in must have opt-out link
   - "Contact us to delete your data (process takes 7 business days)"

### WhatsApp Template Rules
- ✅ Consent templates: "User explicitly agreed to receive offers"
- ❌ No unsolicited bulk sends
- Each template must cite consent date + source

### Implementation
- Add privacy banner to `contact/` pages
- Include consent fields in all forms (CRM-synced)
- Link to updated `privacy/` policy (must cite Amendment 13)
- Consent receipts sent to user email
